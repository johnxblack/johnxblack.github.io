Born in West Virginia, raised in Orlando, Florida & currently living bewteen Copenhagen, Denmark & New York City, JOHNXBLACK™ is a mutli-talented visual artist whose style encompasses a range of influences from abstract expressionism to afro-futurism, hardcore & punk music, southern trap rap, film / cinema, to quantum physics & fashion. A majority of his current fine artwork consists of oils & acrylics on canvas, illustration & sculpture. Through his iconic work he attempts to bring you into the complex visions, moods & atmospheres that he experiences when peering into his mind's eye. Each piece is a one of a kind work that captures a unique moment in space & time and can never be recreated as they come from a freestyle / stream of consciousness process of creation. These surreal & enigmatic visions provide a glimpse into his's memories, traumas, childhood and even precognitions of the future.
